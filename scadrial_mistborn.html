The world of Mistborn

Scadrial is the planet or system on which the Mistborn and Mistborn Era Two series take place. The land is dominated by a red sky with falling ash created by the nearby Ash Mounds, dormant volcanoes that are constantly spewing ash into the sky and blanketing everything in soot, and ruled by a cruel and tyrannical god king known as the Lord Ruler. Under the rule of the Lord Ruler, the common people, known as the Ska, are oppressed and abused by the noble class who run the commercial interests of the Final Empire under the watch of the Lord Ruler and his Canton of Inquisition. At night, the air is filled with mist, obscuring everything in deep white clouds of fog that hide all from sight. Some are said to be blessed with magic powers from this mist, granting them strength, agility, and other magic powers. These are the Mistborn. The story of Mistborn follows Vin, a Ska urchin who is taken in by a thieving crew with a mission: kill the Lord Ruler, and steal his treasury. The story is thrilling, featuring many lovable characters, with twists and turns that any reader of fantasy will love.

Vin

Vin is the main character of the Mistborn series. Born a Ska urchin, she has learned how to survive in a harsh and unforgiving world in the capital city of Luthadel working as a beggar and within thieving crews.

Steel Inquisitor

The Steel Inquisitors are the main police force of the Lord Ruler in his Final Empire. As members of the Steel Ministry, these monsters enforce the will of the Lord Ruler through blood and terror.

The Lord Ruler

The Lord Ruler is the king and ruler of the Last Empire. He is The Sliver of Infinity, and he rules with absolute power and authority. Blessed with immortality, incredible strength, speed, and other magical abilities, he has ruled over all that has been known for the last 1000 years with absolute power and authority.
